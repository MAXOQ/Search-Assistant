# Search-Assistant
A Firefox-based AI assistant that will help you search for websites with the information you need, not give an AI overview. 

## How to use
After adding to Firefox, click the magyfying glass on the top right of your screen. It will pop up with a search bar. Enter your search and click the "search" button. In a few seconds the website will open. 

## How to format a search
It is better to ask a question than to state keywords as you could in google. For example, say "how to bake a cake" or even "cake instructions" as opposed to "bake cake".
