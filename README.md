# Search-Assistant
A Firefox-based AI assistant that will help you search for websites with the information you need, not give an AI overview.

## Use case
This app is designed for use when you need detailed information that you wouldn't trust Google's overview with, such as “how to bake a cake” or a specific research article. It can also be used to find a specific company's or a person's website. 

## How to use
After adding to Firefox, click the magnifying glass on the top right of your screen. It will pop up with a search bar. Enter your search and click the “search” button. In a few seconds, the website will open.

## How to format a search
It is better to ask a question than to state keywords as you could in Google. For example, say “how to bake a cake” or even “cake instructions” as opposed to “bake cake”.  
You may also want to specify what you are doing with the item being searched, for example, if you search for “ballpoint pens” it could take you to the Wikipedia page or Amazon. In this case, you may want to say “what are ballpoint pens” or “buying ballpoint pens.”
